Daily trader workflow

Section 18 — GMT-anchored routine. Links open app pages.

Pre-session (06:30 GMT — ~30 min before London)
  1. /monitor — all systems OK?
  2. /data — load MT5 EURUSD M15 (today).
  3. /risk — funded status: daily DD remaining. If daily loss > 50% of daily limit → stop for the day.
  4. /regimes — detect current regime.
    • Q4 — no trading today.
    • Q3 — check proximity to Asian range extremes.
    • Q1 — check EMA alignment on the regime page.
London open kill zone (07:00–09:00 GMT)
  1. Watch for Q3_M04 or Q1_M04 (kill zone modifier active).
  2. /strategies — read signal (or POST /api/strategies/run-signal).
    • BUY/SELL and RR ≥ 2.5 → proceed.
    • NONE → no trade; observe.
  3. /risk — position size from equity + stop distance (from signal stop).
  4. Cost guard: spread percentile < 80.
  5. Execute (paper or manual).
  6. Log entry — /journal.
NY open kill zone (12:00–13:00 GMT)

Same flow as London. Second-highest-probability window. Often Q3_M04 or Q1_M04 / Q1_M05.

Post-session (after 17:00 GMT)
  1. /journal — review what fired.
  2. Trail stops on profitable positions.
  3. Close all by 21:00 GMT where rules require (e.g. funded — avoid bad rollover).
Weekly (Friday)
  1. /research — backtest validated regimes; watch for WR decay.
  2. Rolling edge monitor — compare to journal outcomes.
Monthly
  1. Full backtest snapshot (~12 months) where data allows.
  2. /analytics — quant review.
  3. Enable/disable strategies from measured evidence only — enable if WR within ±10% of documented prior range.
