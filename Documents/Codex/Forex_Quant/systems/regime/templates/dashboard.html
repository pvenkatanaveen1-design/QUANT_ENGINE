Reference: Key Thresholds & Formulas

What drives regime labels (from systems/regime/service.py + config/regimes.yaml) and signal templates (signal_library_section5.py). Feature math: Wilder (1978) ADX, Kaufman (1995) efficiency ratio. Strategy evidence tags often cite Menkhoff et al. (2012) for FX momentum / trend persistence.

Regime decision tree (base quadrant)

Evaluate in order (first match sets base). Thresholds default from config unless noted.

1. Data quality bad → Q4 (block)
2. Spread percentile ≥ critical (default 95) → Q4 — cost / liquidity stress
3. Jump z > shock (default 3.0) → Q4 — gap / shock proxy
4. Trend if ER ≥ efficiency_ratio_min (default 0.35) AND ADX ≥ adx_trend_min (default 22) — ER: Kaufman (1995)
   • If (high vol: vol pct > 70 OR ATR% ≥ 0.0015) OR extreme vol (pct ≥ 90 OR ATR% ≥ 0.004) → Q2
   • Else → Q1 (trend, normal vol)
5. Else if Range: ER ≤ efficiency_ratio_range_max (default 0.25) AND ADX ≤ adx_range_max (default 18) AND not high-vol bucket → Q3
6. Else if extreme vol and not trending → Q4
7. Else → Q4 transition / mixed

Risk & metrics (formulas)

  • Position size (lots-style): (Account × Risk% / StopDistanceInPips) × PipValue
  • R-multiple (long): (ExitPrice − EntryPrice) / StopDistance — short: mirror with stop direction
  • Sharpe (on R list in engine): (mean(R) / std(R)) × √252 — annualization assumption
  • Profit factor: GrossWins / GrossLosses (losses as positive sum)
  • Expectancy ($ or R): (WinRate × AvgWin) − (LossRate × AvgLoss)
