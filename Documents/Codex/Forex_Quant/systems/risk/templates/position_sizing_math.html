Kelly & position sizing

Section 20 — theory and prop-safe practice. Live sizing in the app uses /risk + config/risk_rules.yaml; regime multipliers live in config/signal_routing.yaml.

Kelly criterion (fractional) [A]
f* = (p × b − q) / b

p = win rate
b = avg_win / avg_loss (payoff / R multiple)
q = 1 − p

Example Q3_M04 (illustrative): p = 0.65, b = 3.0, q = 0.35

f* = (0.65×3 − 0.35) / 3 = 1.6/3 ≈ 0.533 → 53.3% of equity per trade (full Kelly)

Full Kelly is too aggressive for prop firms (variance → gambler’s ruin). Use quarter Kelly [Q] (AQR-style fractional): f_quarter ≈ 0.533 / 4 ≈ 13.3% — still often too high → cap at 2% maximum risk per trade vs equity after all adjustments.

Regime-adjusted sizing (multiply base risk %)

Applied in routing as position_size_multiplier_for_regime:

  • Q1 confirmed: 1.0× [base]
  • Q2 high vol: 0.5× [A][Q]
  • Q3 range: 0.75× [P]
  • Q4 defensive: 0.0× (no trade)
  • Q1_M03 (Asia): 0.5×
  • Q2_M08 (news): 0.35× (also Q2_M09 in config)
  • Ambiguous tape: 0.5× [P] — use judgment; not all cases have a dedicated override.
  • Crisis / extreme vol (e.g. ATR > 2.5× baseline): 0.25× [P] — operator rule; wire in risk layer if you codify a threshold.
Prop firm safety rules
  • • Daily DD buffer: use at most ~50% of the daily loss limit as “room” before standing down.
  • • Total DD buffer: stop trading by ~70% of max drawdown rule (keep ~30% buffer).
  • • Consecutive losses: 2 losses in the same session → stop for day [P].
  • • Correlated exposure: e.g. EUR+GBP+AUD all vs USD counts as one thematic bet → cap total bundle risk (e.g. ~3% portfolio) [A][P].
