Strategy enablement gate

Section 19 — no strategy advances without evidence. Defaults: enabled: false, status: not_tested until you promote manually in the registry.

Do not skip steps. Each gate exists because edge cases would have cost real money.

Move to backtested
  • □ Backtest on real data: ≥ 30 closed trades for this regime (strategy + regime pair).
  • □ Measured WR within ±10% of documented prior range.
  • □ Profit factor > 1.2.
  • □ Expectancy_r > 0.
  • □ Max drawdown < 50% of prop account daily limit.
  • □ Manually set in config/strategy_registry.yaml: status: backtested.
Move to paper_approved
  • □ Status must already be backtested.
  • □ paper_mode: true in config/app.yaml.
  • □ Manually set: status: paper_approved.
  • □ Manually set: enabled: true (only after the above).
Move to live_approved
  • □ 100 paper trades completed for this strategy.
  • □ Paper WR within ±5% of backtest WR.
  • □ Manually set: status: live_approved.
  • □ live_allowed: true in registry for that row (manual only).
  • □ Operator sign-off recorded (your process / document).

Related: /strategies (registry), /research (evidence), approval audit via POST /api/strategies/{id}/approval-preview.
